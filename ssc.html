Work at the Student Startup Center

Summary

The Student Startup Center (SSC) is an on-campus makerspace that provides UC Davis students with an opportunity to experience the entrepreneurial process while developing, refining, and prototyping their ideas for technology startups. We provide many resources such as 3D printers, virtual reality headsets, a laser cutter, and guidance for potential entrepreneurial ventures.

I currently work at the center as a Makerspace Manager, in which I work daily shifts of maintaining the center itself, completing 3D print requests, and putting on events/workshops such as Pitches & Popcorn, an annual internship panel, and some software-based workshops.

Skills and Technology Involved

Leadership | Public Speaking | Event Organization | Team Collaboration | 3D Printing
